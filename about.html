About

Besides programming, I live a pretty active lifestyle. I enjoy exploring what the world has to offer starting in the Pacific NorthWest. I hike regardless of season, rock climb (when it's not raining or freezing), backpacking and camping. I hope to get into mountaineering once I've established a career and could actually afford the gear.

I love the outdoors, but I am definitely a city girl. Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, I cannot picture myself outside of the city for long. I love the selection of goods that cities have to offer. I am a huge consumer of FOOD. I'm always out and about, hanging out with friends, and eating. I try not to spend too much time indoors since I do that during the rainy season anyway.

Fun Fact: I got into rock climbing while watching American Ninja Warrior. I was amazed at how strong the contestants' upper body and decided I wanted a stronger upper body. You won't see me on the show, maybe later, because I found out I could eat pretty much anything if I just rock climb.
